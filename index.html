Who Will come out on Top?

The question that has been in every gamer's mind is, is it worth having a PC (Personal Computer) or a console. Today, we will look at both sides on the pricing and the hardware of both sides. This video will tell you everything you want to know about both sides in a very quick matter.

Pricing

When looking into a buying or building a pc, it will hurt a dent on your wallet for a decent and suffiecent computer. The price mark on computer will be in the range of $1,000 to $2,000. Now for games, its a bit cheaper on the pc hovering around $40 dollars.

The price on console has changed this year. Before with other console it would usually cost around $300, but now it's at $500. Pricing for games is at $60 dollars
